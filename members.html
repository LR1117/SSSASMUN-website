Member Roles & Responsibilities

Different ways to contribute to the club

Delegate

Represents assigned countries in conference committees. Researches policy positions, drafts resolutions, and debates with other delegations.

Committee Chair

Leads committee proceedings, maintains order, and guides discussions. Requires advanced knowledge of UN procedures and conference rules.

Crisis Committee Actor

Plays roles in crisis committees that simulate real-world diplomatic emergencies. Adds drama and realism to conference experiences.

Volunteer Helper

Assists with conference logistics, setup, and coordination. Great for those still learning about the Model UN process.
