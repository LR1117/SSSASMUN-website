Our Club Mission

Our Model UN club is dedicated to fostering understanding of international affairs, developing critical thinking skills, and providing students with a platform to engage in meaningful debate on global issues through simulation of United Nations proceedings.

Since our founding, we've grown to become one of the most active clubs at our school, with members competing at conferences across the region and state. We're committed to developing the next generation of diplomats, leaders, and informed global citizens.
